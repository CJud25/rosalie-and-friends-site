Foster a Dog

We're a foster-based rescue — no kennels, no facility. Every dog we save lives in a home like yours until they find their own. That home could be yours.

Three Ways to Foster

Different dogs need different things. There's a fit for your household and your season of life.

⌂

Adoption Fosters

House a healthy, adoptable dog while we find their forever family. Usually a few weeks to a few months — and yes, "foster wins" happen all the time.

Fostering Costs You Nothing but Love

Here's exactly how the responsibilities split.

We Provide

  • All veterinary care, medications, and treatments
  • Food, crates, and supplies whenever you need them
  • A rescue team you can reach with questions, day or night
  • A backup plan — if life changes, the dog always comes back to us

You Provide

  • A safe, loving, indoor home
  • Daily care, patience, and routine
  • Rides to vet appointments when you're able
  • Photos and updates that help your foster get noticed

Foster Questions, Answered

Does fostering cost me anything?

No. The rescue covers vet care, medications, and supplies. Many fosters chip in food or toys because they want to, but it's never required.

Can I adopt my foster dog?

Absolutely — we call it a "foster win," and it's one of our favorite outcomes. Fosters get first right of adoption on their dog.

What if it's not a good fit?

Then we rematch, no guilt attached. An honest "this isn't working" helps us learn what the dog needs. The dog always has a place with the rescue.

What if I travel or go on vacation?

Tell us the dates and we'll arrange coverage with another foster. Life doesn't stop because you foster — we plan around it.

I've never had a senior or special-needs dog. Can I still fospice foster?

Yes. We walk you through everything — medications, comfort care, what to watch for — and our fospice fosters support each other constantly. Willingness matters more than experience.
