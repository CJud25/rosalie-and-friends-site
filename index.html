How Adoption Works

We keep things simple so you can focus on what matters — finding your dog.

01

Apply Online

Fill out our short adoption application. We review every application personally, usually within 48 hours.

02

Meet Your Match

We'll arrange a meet-and-greet with dogs that fit your lifestyle, family, and home environment.

03

Take Them Home

Once approved, your new best friend comes home with you. We provide support every step of the way.

Our Signature Program

“Fospice and fixer uppers.”

Senior dogs are too often left behind when their people pass away or enter care. Our fospice program places them with forever fosters who love them through their final chapter — while we cover every vet bill along the way.
