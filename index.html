How Adoption Works

We keep things simple so you can focus on what matters — finding your dog.

01

Apply Online

Fill out our adoption application. Our board personally reviews every one, then follows up with a phone call.

02

Meet Your Match

We'll arrange a home visit and meet-and-greet so you and your matched dog can connect first.

03

Take Them Home

Once approved, your new best friend comes home — and if life ever changes, we will always take them back.

Our Signature Program

“Fospice and fixer uppers.”

Senior pets are too often left behind. Our fospice program takes them from the shelter and into loving homes through end of life — comfort, dignity, and a family for their final chapter.
