The Adoption Process

Simple, transparent, and designed to find the best match for both you and your new dog.

01

Submit an Application

Fill out our brief application. We ask about your home, lifestyle, and experience with dogs to find your best match.

02

Application Review

Our board reviews every application, followed by a phone call to talk through the details.

03

Meet & Greet

We arrange a home visit and meet-and-greet with your matched dog so you can connect before committing.

04

Welcome Home

Once approved, your dog comes home with you — and if life ever changes, we will always take them back.
